Murder Mystery Top-Down

How to Play:

  • Murderer: Eliminate everyone. Use Left Click / Action to stab. Press SPACE to throw. Your identity is hidden until you attack!
  • Sheriff: Find and shoot the Murderer. Use Mouse/Tap to aim, Left Click/Action to shoot.
  • Innocent: Survive and collect coins. If Sheriff dies, pick up the gun!
  • Controls: WASD/Arrows to move. Mouse to aim. Mobile uses on-screen controls.
